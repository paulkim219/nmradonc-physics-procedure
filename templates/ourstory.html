Bridging AI & Radiation Oncology

A Northwestern University Practicum Project

Our Mission

With the rise of AI in healthcare, we believe that AI can be a powerful tool to help in all aspects of Radiation Oncology. By building a single platform where doctors, nurses, students, and patients can all interact, we can help streamline every process of Radiation Oncology with the power of AI.

Our Offerings

We are currently offering a single platform where doctors, physicians, nurses, students, and patients can all interact with the power of AI.

  • Physicians: Our platform will allow physicians to ask questions about Radiation Oncology trials, where our AI will give back any guidelines and predicted outcomes of the different trials.
  • Nurses: Our platform will be able to aid in question generation for our patients to help them understand their treatment better.
  • Students: With our RAG database that contains all guidelines and trial outcomes about Radiation Oncology, students can take quizes to test their knowledge.
  • Doctors: With their expertise, doctors will be able to see any respones from the AI and provide feedback to the AI to improve its accuracy.
  • Patients: In coordination with Northwestern Medicine, our patients will be able to interact with AI to schedule appointments and answer any personalized questions about their treatment.
